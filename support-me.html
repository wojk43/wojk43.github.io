Support

Hi! Here's how you're currently able to support me:

  • Report any mistakes on the website

    • Sometimes, there are typos or links take you to the wrong place. Maybe a link doesn't exist at all in fact. Please report this if you find anything :)

  • Report any errors made in the handouts

    • I create the examples from scratch and see what would work and sometimes when working forward again I make some mistakes...

  • Report any mistakes made in the past paper solution repository

    • I'm slowly starting to work on this... but once again, I make a lot of mistakes, especially arithmetic mistakes. Sometimes, there may even be a logical flaw. If you believe that I did something wrong, please contact me!

  • Submit latex versions of my past paper solutions

    • As I'm working on the repository, I don't have enough time to latex my solutions. Alas, that's not the reason I decided to work on this in the first place but it doesn't hurt to post these solutions online

  • Submit your own past paper solutions!

    • I can't get to all past papers, and some modules I don't even do. For example, it would be a reach for me to try to complete a Mathematical Biology paper whereas other people may have already one the papers

  • Contact me with any suggestions you have pertaining the website!

    • I want to make this website as user-friendly and inviting as possible. The aim is to help people and be able to express myself. If you know a method with which I could provide better help then please let me know!
