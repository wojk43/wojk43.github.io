About me

My name is Wojciech Kowalczyk, and I am a student entering my final year of my mathematics masters degree at Durham University.

Over the past few years, I have been deeply involved in advanced mathematics, exploring areas such as algebraic number theory, elliptic curves and modular forms (Silverman, Diamond & Shurman etc.). While I originally intended to pursue an academic career, I have recently decided to pivot into software engineering, where I can apply my problem solving skills to another field.

In addition to my study, I have been involved in many mathematical competitions on both national and international levels.

Software engineering

I am now actively developing my expertise in software engineering, with interests including Python, HTML, CSS, Javascript, Django and more.
