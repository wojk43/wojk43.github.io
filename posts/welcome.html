This page was created to talk about the purpose of the website, what motivates it, what's on it etc.

Purpose

The purpose of the website consists of four main things.

  • To share insights to interesting things that I found
  • To be able to express myself freely and talk about things I enjoy
  • Provide resources and advice to high-achieving upcoming undergraduates
  • Provide resources to current undergraduadtes
In summary, help people and express myself freely.

Motivation

I would describe myself as quite a studious person. The fact that I enjoy math by itself could be quite the tell. As a consequence of this, I have a lot of practice, sit a lot of papers, learn a lot of things etc. that otherwise would take a very long time to do. With that in mind, it does not take a lot of effort to transfer some of that to other people and help out. After all, we should help each other out when we can :)

Contents

As of right now, the content of the website is quite limited, but here is what is available as of right now:

  • My personal blog
  • An interview page to aid highschoolers with their Oxbridge interviews
  • A STEP page to aid highschoolers in STEP preparation
  • An about me page to get to know me
  • A support me page so you can find out ways to support this website :)

Future plans for the website

Of course, you may have noticed that there are a lot of unavailable parts of the websites... Some of these may provide hints of whats to come already, however here is a complete list of what I have planned for now:

  • Writing an MAT section
  • Adding additional STEP resources and sorting them by category
  • Making a contact me section on the right-hand side
  • Filling the competition page, slowly but surely, or maybe revamping it
  • Creating a solution repository for past papers and also problems sheets I personally created
  • Uploading resources here. Handouts, problem sheets and video explanations.
